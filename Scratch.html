Top 5 Destinations

  1. Portugal



    2. Portugal is a breathtaking country. It is very safe for the most part and the people are so kind. You can find many scenic and peaceful spots.






  2. London

    3. London is one of the worlds busiest cities! There are so many locals, not to mention people traveling for business and tourists. London is a very fun city for history-buffs, foodies, or people looking to have a fun night out.












  3. Brazil

    4. Everything is bigger in Brazil! The food portions... well that's about it. Brazil is such a lively place to visit. Coupled with delicious food, upbeat music and dancing, and scenic beaches, Brazil is an amazing destination.
